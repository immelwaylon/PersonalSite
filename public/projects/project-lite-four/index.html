Extended Summary
#

The original files I used were a high poly moai stone model, a low poly moai stone model, and a generic keycap model I found. I combined all of them in tinkercad by hollowing out the moai and placing the keycap stem in the middle. I had to adjust tolerances, scale things, and reshape everything a few times to get 3d prints of the file to fit correctly on a standard keyboard. The final product is here, with a few versions that work on different types of keyboards.
